Support datasets List

  • For every dataset sequence, we provide a script for replaying and remapping.
  • For EuRoC datasets, we provide groundtruth.txt in tum format, all need to do is download the sequence.
  • For TUM RGB-D datasets, just download the sequennce and groundtruth, and rename groundtruth file to groundtruth.txt.
  • For KITTI datasets, we provide the groundtruth, all need to do is download the raw data and use kitti2bag to transform it to bag.
  • For EuRoC and TUM RGB-D datastes, we provide download script: SLAM_Hive/slam_hive_datasets/download_all.py.
